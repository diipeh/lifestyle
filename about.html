We we are

Welcome to Lifestyle Disability Services (LDS), where our unwavering belief in the power of inclusivity and the celebration of individual abilities shapes the heart of our mission. At LDS, we are driven by a profound commitment to empowering lives and enriching communities through person-centered support for individuals with diverse abilities. Our journey is a testament to the transformative impact of fostering independence, creating meaningful connections, and championing inclusion in every facet of life.

In the vibrant tapestry of LDS, each individual's unique path is not just acknowledged but celebrated. We recognize and honor the rich diversity of abilities, acknowledging that every person contributes uniquely to the broader community. Our mission goes beyond mere support; it is a dedication to cultivating an environment where every individual thrives and is encouraged to pursue their aspirations.

Join us on this purpose-driven journey as we navigate a world of possibilities, committed to breaking down barriers that may hinder personal growth and development. At LDS, we believe that building a community that embraces diversity is not only a noble pursuit but a fundamental aspect of creating a thriving society. In this community, differences are not only accepted but embraced as sources of strength, resilience, and inspiration.

Together, we build a community that empowers each person to lead a life that is not just fulfilling but purposeful. We understand that true empowerment extends beyond mere assistance; it involves recognizing and harnessing the inherent potential within each individual. As we work towards this collective vision, our commitment to championing diversity, fostering inclusion, and promoting independence remains unwavering.

Why choose us?

Our Approach

What sets us apart is our person-centered approach. We recognize that each person we serve is on a distinct journey, with individual aspirations, challenges, and dreams. Our dedicated team works collaboratively with individuals, their families, and the community to tailor our support services to meet specific needs, fostering a sense of independence and self-determination.

Core Values

At the core of Lifestyle Disability Services are values that guide our every action. We champion empowerment, inclusivity, and quality care. Our commitment to integrity, collaboration, and continuous improvement ensures that our services not only meet but exceed the expectations of those we serve.

Inclusive Community

We believe in the power of community, where diversity is not only accepted but celebrated. Our programs and initiatives are designed to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and embraced for their unique identity.

Advocacy for Change

Beyond our role as service providers, we are advocates for change. Lifestyle Disability Services actively engages in breaking down societal barriers, promoting accessibility, and influencing a more inclusive future for all.

Your Partner in the Journey
