Current Vacancies

  • Job Advertisement: Support Worker Positions
  • About Us: Support Worker Positions
  • Position: Support Worker
  • Location: Canberra
  • Responsibilities: As a Support Worker at Lifestyle Disability Services, you will:
    • Provide person-centered support to individuals with disabilities in various aspects of daily living.
    • Assist with personal care, meal preparation, and household tasks to foster independence.
    • Facilitate community engagement and social inclusion activities.
    • Implement individualized support plans and promote skill development.
    • Collaborate with a multidisciplinary team to ensure holistic care and support.
    • Maintain accurate and timely documentation of support provided.
  • Requirements: As a Support Worker at Lifestyle Disability Services, you will:
    • A genuine passion for supporting individuals with disabilities.
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
    • Ability to work collaboratively in a team and adapt to changing needs.
    • Compassion, patience, and a positive attitude.
    • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and evenings.
    • Certificate III or IV in Disability Support or a related field (desirable).
  • Benefits:
    • Competitive salary based on qualifications and experience.
    • Ongoing professional development opportunities.
    • Supportive and inclusive work environment.
    • Opportunities for career advancement.
    • Rewarding and meaningful work with a positive impact.
  • How to Apply:
    • If you are enthusiastic about making a difference and meet the requirements, please submit your resume and a cover letter addressing how your skills align with the position.
