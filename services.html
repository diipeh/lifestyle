Independent Supported Living

Our Independent Supported Living services are crafted to empower individuals with disabilities to lead autonomous lives within their community. We offer personalized support plans that address daily living activities, community integration, and skill development. Our services encompass assistance with daily tasks, community engagement, and holistic health support, promoting self-sufficiency.

Key Features

Customized Support

Personalized assessment leads to tailored support plans, ensuring individual needs and goals are met.

Daily Living Assistance

Support with meal preparation, personal hygiene, and household chores promotes self-sufficiency.

Community Integration

Facilitating participation in social events and local gatherings fosters a sense of community belonging.

Skill Development Programs

Targeted programs enhance communication, budgeting, and self-advocacy skills.

Health and Wellness Focus

Assistance with healthcare appointments and medication management prioritizes well-being.

Flexibility and Adaptability

Services are flexible, allowing for adjustments to support plans as needs evolve.

Community-Based Housing Support

Collaboration with housing providers assists in finding suitable accommodation.

Holistic Approach

Fostering self-determination, decision-making, and personal growth contributes to holistic empowerment.

Family and Caregiver Collaboration

Collaborative efforts with families ensure a unified and supportive approach to well-being.

Our commitment is to create environments where individuals can lead meaningful, self-directed lives, embodying our dedication to independence, dignity, and community for those we support.

Short Term Accommodation

At Lifestyle Disability Services, our Short-Term Accommodation services are designed to offer individuals with disabilities a supportive and enriching temporary living environment. We understand the importance of providing a comfortable and inclusive space that caters to varying needs while fostering independence.

Key Features

Residential Comfort and Accessibility

Our short-term accommodation facilities are designed for comfort and accessibility, ensuring a welcoming and inclusive environment for individuals with diverse needs.

Personalized Support Plans

Each resident benefits from a personalized support plan tailored to their unique requirements, encompassing daily living activities, personal care, and specific support needs.

Recreational and Leisure Activities

Lifestyle Disability Services offers a range of recreational and leisure activities to promote engagement and enjoyment during the stay. These activities are adapted to individual preferences and abilities.

Skilled and Compassionate Staff

Our dedicated staff members are skilled and compassionate, providing round-the-clock support to ensure the well-being and comfort of residents.

Transition Support

For individuals transitioning between different environments or services, our short-term accommodation services act as a supportive bridge. We assist in ensuring a smooth transition and continuity of care.

Access to Community Events

Residents have the opportunity to participate in community events and outings, enhancing social connections and providing a sense of community integration.

Family and Caregiver Involvement

We recognize the importance of involving families and caregivers in the short-term accommodation experience. Open communication ensures a collaborative approach to care and support.

Nutritious Meal Plans

Our facilities provide nutritious and well-balanced meal plans, catering to dietary preferences and requirements, contributing to the overall health and vitality of residents.

Focus on Independence Skills

Lifestyle Disability Services emphasizes the development of independence skills during the short-term stay, preparing individuals for increased autonomy in their daily lives.

Flexible Duration of Stay

Whether for respite care, transitional support, or other short-term needs, the duration of stay is flexible and tailored to the individual's requirements.

Quality Assurance and Continuous Improvement

Our commitment to quality assurance involves regular assessments and feedback mechanisms to continually enhance the quality of our short-term accommodation services.

Through our Short-Term Accommodation services, Lifestyle Disability Services aims to provide a secure, supportive, and engaging environment where individuals can experience temporary respite or transition smoothly between different phases of their lives.
