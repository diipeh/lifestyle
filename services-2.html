Direct Support Services at Lifestyle Disability Services

Person-Centered Support

Tailored support plans based on individual needs, preferences, and goals.

Daily Living Assistance

Hands-on support for personal care, meal preparation, and household tasks to foster self-sufficiency.

Community Inclusion

Active engagement in community activities, events, and outings to promote social belonging.

Skill Development and Training

Programs focusing on essential life skills like communication, budgeting, and problem-solving for enhanced independence.

Health and Well-being Support

Prioritization of overall health through assistance with healthcare appointments, medication management, and promotion of healthy lifestyles.

Flexible and Adaptable Assistance

Adaptable services to meet evolving needs, working closely with individuals and their support networks.

Assistive Technology Integration

Implementation of assistive technology solutions catering to specific needs and preferences.

Family and Caregiver Collaboration

Collaborative care approach involving close coordination with families and caregivers.

Crisis Intervention and Emergency Response

Trained team capable of handling crisis situations, ensuring immediate assistance and safety.

Quality Assurance and Continuous Improvement

Commitment to maintaining high-quality services through regular quality assurance activities and feedback mechanisms, ensuring ongoing excellence.

Our Direct Support Services are crafted with a commitment to fostering independence, inclusivity, and an enriched quality of life for individuals with disabilities. By prioritizing personalized care and comprehensive support, we create a nurturing environment that empowers those we serve.

Group activity and program

At Lifestyle Disability Services, our Group Activity and Program Services are crafted to offer a dynamic and inclusive environment where individuals with disabilities can engage in meaningful group activities, fostering social connections, skill development, and personal growth.

Key Features

Diverse Activity Programs

We offer a wide range of group activities catering to various interests, including arts and crafts, recreational outings, educational workshops, and fitness programs. Our diverse offerings ensure there's something for everyone.

Tailored Programs for Varied Abilities

Recognizing the unique abilities of each participant, our programs are tailored to accommodate diverse skill levels, ensuring an inclusive and supportive atmosphere for all.

Social Connection and Community Building

Group activities provide opportunities for social interaction and community building. Participants can form friendships, share experiences, and build a supportive network within the Lifestyle Disability Services community.

Skill Enhancement Initiatives

Our programs focus on skill enhancement, covering a range of areas such as communication, problem-solving, creativity, and physical well-being. These initiatives contribute to the holistic development of participants.

Qualified and Supportive Staff

Our qualified and supportive staff members are dedicated to creating a positive and engaging atmosphere during group activities. They provide guidance, encouragement, and assistance as needed.

Regularly Scheduled Events

We organize regularly scheduled events and programs, ensuring consistency and providing participants with a sense of routine and anticipation for upcoming activities.

Celebration of Achievements

Milestones and achievements are celebrated within the group, fostering a sense of accomplishment and boosting self-esteem among participants.

Flexible Participation Options

Lifestyle Disability Services offers flexible participation options, allowing individuals to choose activities that align with their preferences and availability.

Family and Caregiver Involvement

We encourage family and caregiver involvement in group activities, fostering a sense of inclusion and connection beyond the program setting.

Community Integration Initiatives

Group activities extend beyond our facilities, with initiatives designed to integrate participants into the broader community. This may include outings, partnerships with local organizations, and community engagement events.

Continuous Feedback Loop

A continuous feedback loop ensures that our group activity and program services evolve based on the preferences and needs of participants, promoting a responsive and adaptive approach.

Through our Group Activity and Program Services, Lifestyle Disability Services aims to create an inclusive, vibrant, and supportive community where individuals with disabilities can participate in enriching group activities, develop new skills, and build lasting connections.
