Support Coordination

Personalized Support Plans

Individualized assessments form the basis for creating personalized support plans.

Navigating the Disability Support System

Experienced coordinators guide individuals through the disability support system, ensuring access to the right services, funding, and resources.

Advocacy and Empowerment

Empowering individuals to advocate for their needs and choices, fostering self-determination.

Goal Setting and Planning

Working closely to set achievable goals and develop comprehensive plans aligned with aspirations.

Monitoring and Reviewing Progress

Continuous monitoring and reviews to ensure services align with evolving needs and goals.

Capacity Building

Focus on building individuals' capacity for independent support management, including skill development.

Crisis Intervention and Problem Resolution

Proactive crisis intervention and problem resolution support for seamless continuity of care.

Flexible and Person-Centered Approach

Flexible, person-centered services adapted to specific needs, preferences, and cultural backgrounds.

Continuous Collaboration and Communication

Open communication and collaboration with individuals, families, and stakeholders for a cohesive support network.

Our Support Coordination services are dedicated to empowering individuals with disabilities through personalized guidance and effective navigation of the support system, enhancing their quality of life.
